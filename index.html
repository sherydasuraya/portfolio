About Me

A third-year student at University Malaysia Pahang who graduated from aircraft maintenance and now moving into the automation industry.
A daring individual who enjoys travelling and fishing. A person who enjoys trying new things like archery, kayaking, camping, and many others while still being able to concentrate on her academics, keep up her grades, and be an active participant in a number of clubs.
